      There are two types of privilege: the presence of helping hands or the
      absence of kicks.


      Helping hands are those [advantages](#media-02-coups_de_pouce) that the
      world offers us to help us go further and faster. Kicks are all those
      [obstacles](#media-02-coups_de_pied) the world places in our path to make
      us go slower and less far. One is positive, the other negative.


      I often use [the metabolism metaphor](#media-02-metabolisme) to illustrate
      these dynamics. Imagine you want to gain weight. If your metabolism is fast,
      you will have more difficulty gaining it (kick) whereas you will find it
      much easier if your metabolism is slow (helping hand).


      Both are difficult to recognize and give us the impression that we live in
      a system where everyone has equal chances. A person born Swiss will never
      be aware of the additional administrative burdens that the different
      [residence permits](#media-02-charge_administrative_permis) entail (kicks),
      just as they will not realize that their CV will be more attractive due to
      [their nationality](#media-02-CVs) or [their
      gender](https://www.forbes.com/sites/tomaspremuzic/2022/03/08/equality-isnt-exceptional-women-getting-ahead-but-incompetent-men-falling-behind/)
      (helping hand).


      This inability to notice the kicks and helping hands applies to all forms
      of discrimination. This is what is shown, for example, by the 2023 gfs.bern
      survey for SSR on the [perception of gender equality](#media-02-enquete_RTS)
      in Switzerland, where more than 50% of men stated that men and women are
      treated equally, compared to about 25% for women.


      In the end, people who believe in this misconception have not been led to
      reflect on all these dynamics of discrimination and invisible favoritism.
      This does not mean they have not made efforts. It simply means that their
      efforts have been amplified (or have not been diminished).


      A plane will still have to use its engines to move forward. But depending
      on whether the winds are favorable or not, it will take more or less time
      to [reach its destination](#media-02-airplane_wind).


      The goal of the concept of privilege is not to make privileged people feel
      guilty, but to understand that the systems our societies have built create
      inequalities and therefore need to be adjusted, modified, or replaced. This
      [TikTok](#media-02-TikTok_on_privileges) in English provides a good
      illustration.
