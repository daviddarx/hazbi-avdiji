  - content: "Denying the difficulties others experience is one of the basic tactics used to sweep problems under the rug when we have something to answer for. This is typically the [kind of reaction](https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2024-06-24/corporate-women-s-gains-fall-victim-to-anti-woke-dei-backlash?embedded-checkout=true) a [very male](#media-04-male_lynching) executive committee might have when an internal working group asks for the glass ceiling to be broken for women.\n\nSometimes, we also say this out of surprise. \"It is incredible, until a few years ago nobody said anything and now it is fashionable to complain about everything. Is what minorities say true, or are they just complaining for the sake of complaining?\"\n\nIt is not that minorities were not speaking up before — they were, but in [spaces](#media-04-chez_Moune) where their voices were wanted to be heard, where they felt safe. And today, the public square is finally safe for talking about these things (though that is beginning to change for the worse). And in this public square, everyone hears the grievances, even people who did not want to listen in the first place.\n\nWhat remains for minorities is to explain what they experience in the most rational and scientific way possible (for fear of being accused of making things up). And today, the numbers are plentiful. To cite only those we hear less often:\n\n* How many generations does it take for a person born into a low-income family to reach the average income level in Switzerland? 1? 2? [No… 5](#media-04-Suisse_5_generations)! The social elevator is very slow.\n* [9 out of 10 people](#media-04-Guardian_gender_bias) have sexist biases. Try to find your place in that as a woman…\n* [41% of trans youth](https://transequality.org/sites/default/files/docs/usts/USTS-Full-Report-Dec17.pdf) living in transphobic family settings have suicidal thoughts. This rate drops to 4% when the family is fully accepting of their trans identity.\n\nAnd in the end, are not the conservatives the ones screaming [for nothing](#media-04-war_on)?\n"
