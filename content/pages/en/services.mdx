      My services are built around [three core pillars](#media-services-piliers),
      each designed to address the specific reality and needs of your organization.
      Together, we will identify the most useful and effective approach for you.


      <subtitleTitle subtitle="" title="Pillar 1: Awareness" />


      > Goal: training to understand, learn, and unlearn.


      The content of the training sessions is based on the **misconceptions**
      model. We will choose, based on the reality of your organization and the
      time available for training, a number of misconceptions and themes to cover.


      **Different formats are possible depending on your needs:**


      * Short capsules (\<2h) in the form of lectures or roundtables to introduce
      the topic of D\&I in your organization or spark a discussion on specific
      themes.

      * Half-day to multi-day training sessions and workshops to explore certain
      themes in depth.

      * Training can be delivered in English.


      ![](</uploads/VT_01300 credits.jpg> "2400x1802")


      <subtitleTitle subtitle="" title="Pillar 2: Structural support" />


      > Goal: helping your organization move from intention to action.


      To help you move forward with confidence, I can offer you one or more of
      these services:


      * Diagnosis and analysis of your D\&I needs through an **audit**
      (questionnaires, individual interviews, document analysis)

      * Development of **performance and impact indicators**, evaluation support

      * Development of a **D\&I strategy** aligned with your overall strategy

      * Support for **project management**

      * Management of **change and resistance** among your employees


      <subtitleTitle title="Pillar 3: D&I tool development" subtitle="" />


      > Goal: creating tangible, tailored, and reusable solutions.


      Through a design thinking process, I support you in developing your D\&I
      toolbox, which can include:


      * Conversational agents based on **artificial intelligence** that can serve
      as a D\&I resource, auditor, or even legal advisor

      * Decision matrices, competency profiles, and other useful tools for your
      **recruitment and evaluation processes**

      * Internal **guides** (e.g., inclusive language guide, staff charter, guide
      to applying intersectionality in operations)


      ## Good to know


      * I quickly grasp the nuances of different sectors, as demonstrated by
      mandates I have completed in **fields as diverse as** public administration,
      pharma, culture, hospitality, or research and development.

      * As a **generalist**, I will be your entry point into D\&I. My experience
      has allowed me to build a **network of partners** (experts, associations,
      companies) with whom I collaborate for more specific needs.

      * I offer **preferential rates** for any organization with a social purpose
      and/or a limited budget.
